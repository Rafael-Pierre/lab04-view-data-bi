
Dashboard layout suggestions (pages and visuals):

Page 1 - Caracterizacao do Dataset
- Cards: Total Accidents (13 rows in cleaned CSV), Total Deaths (SUM mortos), Total Vehicles (SUM veiculos)
- Bar chart: Accidents per Year (axis: year)
- Map: Points by latitude/longitude (use latitude & longitude); tooltip: uf, municipio, mortos, feridos
- Table: Top 10 municipalities by number of accidents

Page 2 - Perguntas de Pesquisa (RQs)
- Line chart: Monthly trend (axis: month_year)
- Bar chart: Deaths by State (axis: uf)
- Stacked bar or donut: Accident types (tipo_acidente) distribution
- Heatmap: Hour of day vs Day of week (count of accidents)

Notes:
- The Power Query script in 'powerquery_script_m.txt' is a generic starting point; update the file path to your CSV in Power BI's Advanced Editor.
- The cleaned CSV 'clean_accidents.csv' includes parsed date/year/month and normalized uf/municipio columns.
